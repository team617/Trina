How We Work
Together

Come as you are, with whatever brings you here.

I start working with my clients wherever they feel safe right now. It's not my goal to push boundaries or decide what the next step should be. I trust that when safety and connection are present, transformation begins to unfold in its own perfect timing.

My clients don't need to know what's wrong or why something feels off. We will explore every layer of being through movement, conversation, and awareness so that misalignment naturally starts to reveal itself.

Start to Explore

Group Yoga Sessions

A welcoming space to begin your journey through mindful movement and breath. These sessions offer a gentle introduction to alignment work in a supportive group setting.

Each class is designed to help you reconnect with your body, release tension, and cultivate awareness — all while surrounded by a community of like-minded individuals on their own journeys.

  • Accessible to all levels and bodies
  • Focus on breath, alignment, and presence
  • Small group sizes for personal attention
  • Regular weekly schedule
