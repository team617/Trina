Common Questions

Before We Meet

I'm not sure which service is right for me.

That's exactly what the onboarding call is for. We'll explore together where you are and what might serve you best. There's no pressure to decide anything on the call.

Do I need yoga experience?

No prior experience is needed. My work is accessible to all levels and all bodies. We start wherever you are.

Are sessions online or in-person?

I offer both online and in-person sessions. Online sessions allow us to work together from anywhere in the world, while in-person sessions are available in select locations.

What if I'm not ready for deep work?

That's perfectly okay. We always start where you feel safe. Group yoga sessions are a wonderful way to begin exploring without any pressure. The depth of our work together is always guided by your readiness.
